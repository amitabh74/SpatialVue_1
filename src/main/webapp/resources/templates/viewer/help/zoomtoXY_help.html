Zoom To XY


‘Zoom To XY’  tool allows users to zoom to a location of known coordinates on the map canvas. Click on the tool and application opens ZoomtoXY tab in left panel.


Zoom to specified Co-ordinates


  1. Enter the longitude and latitude of the location.
  2. Click on ‘Zoom To’ button. Application will zoom to the entered location with a pin mark on map.
  3. After entering the coordinates of the location, user clicks on ‘Pan To’ button, application pans the map to that location with a pin mark.
